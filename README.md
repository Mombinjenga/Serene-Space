# Serene Space

[Live Demo](https://serene-space-mentalwellness.netlify.app/)  

**Serene Space** is a web application dedicated to promoting mental wellness and mindfulness. The platform provides users with a calm and organized digital environment where they can explore mental health resources, relaxation exercises, and self-care tips. Its goal is to help users reduce stress, improve focus, and foster a sense of balance in their daily lives.

## About

Serene Space is designed for anyone looking to improve their mental wellbeing through simple, accessible online resources. Users can:

- Access guided relaxation exercises  
- Explore tips for mindfulness and self-care  
- Enjoy a clean, distraction-free interface to focus on mental wellness  

The application emphasizes simplicity, usability, and responsive design for an optimal experience across devices.
